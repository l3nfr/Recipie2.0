Shahi Paneer Recipie

About

It is a classical North Indian rich and creamy curry recipe prepared with a combination of dry fruits with onion sauce and paneer cubes. In Shahi Paneer, the word ‘Shahi’ literally means royal in Hindi, and the reference is attached mainly because of its rich and luxurious profile. It is also believed that the recipe and the gravy sauce were first introduced during the Mughal empires and served to the royal family. Hence, it carries the name and serves it on auspicious and religious occasions. A simple and creamy Shahi Paneer Masala Recipe is prepared in many ways. The most common way is to make it with a combination of nuts like almonds, cashews, and walnuts and diced onions. The tomato is skipped as it does not yield a rich colour, however in some variation, even tomatoes are added. Adding tomatoes will change their colour and texture, and it is typically added in dhaba-style recipes. Further, the curry can also be used as a multi-purpose sauce and other vegetables and meat tenders can be added to the same sauce.

Ingredients

for tomato puree :

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 inch cinnanmon
  • ½ tsp cloves
  • 3 pods cardamom
  • 1 inch ginnger
  • cloves garlic
  • 2 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomato, sliced
  • 2 tbsp badam, blanched
  • ¼ cup curd

for curry :

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ¼ cup cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 20 cubes paneer
  • few kesar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp kasuri methi, crushed
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Firstly, in a pan heat 2 tbsp oil. Take spices and saute on low flame.
  2. Add 1 inch ginger, 4 cloves garlic and 2 onion. Saute until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Also add 2 tomato, 2 tbsp badam and cook till the tomatoes soften.
  4. Cool completely, and transfer to the mixer jar.
  5. Add ¼ cup curd, and grind to smooth paste.
  6. Now sieve the puree to get a silky smooth texture. Keep aside.
  7. In a Kadai, take 2 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp oil. Add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder and ½ tsp cumin powder.
  8. Saute on low flame till the spices turn aromatic.
  9. Add in prepared puree and cook well.
  10. Also add ¼ cup cream, 1 tsp salt and cook till the oil separates.
  11. Now add 20 cubes paneer, few kesar, 1 tsp sugar and mix well.
  12. Simmer for 5 minutes, or until the flavours are well absorbed.
  13. Add 2 tbsp kasuri methi, 2 tbsp coriander and mix well.
  14. Finally, enjoy Shahi Paneer Recipe with roti and naan.
